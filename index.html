  • Sound design

    From early 2017 towards the end of 2019, Escapa Studio was my second home. The recognized music house with Chilean roots helped me develop my skills and career in the sound post production business. Starting as an intern, all the way to earning my place taking care of Studio B. There, I was able to deliver campaigns for Adidas, Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Multicenter, Tigo, JAC and several other local and international brands.

    During a brief stay in Denmark, I met Katarzyna Romatowska, a talented Polish director and writer with whom I had the pleasure to work with in the short film Polaczone. This copy is being shared for reel purposes only, please do not distribute.

    Radiotherapy was an award-winning piece we were happy to be a part of. Helping spread the word about the importance of detecting breast cancer in time.

    Banca Activa, winner of the Forbes Ad of the Year in 2019 was a beautifully crafted campaign for the Bolivian National Bank. And the sound design had to keep up with the incredible work the team put up.

    The Adidas Marathon took place in Santa Cruz de la Sierra, giving us the opportunity to work on a commercial shot between the darkness and the soft light of dawn.

    With 'Even the heaviest of loads' JAC brought a new truck. Having shot several pieces for this campaign, this one brought out a few special traits that I deem worth sharing.

  • Nescafé

    On the 25th of May, 1809 in the city of Chuquisaca, Bolivia (now Sucre) a movement began, rumours spread and people weren't happy.

    The decision was made to gather the town around the main cathedral in the Templo de San Francisco to make a decision. The bell was rung long and hard until everyone could hear it and come around. And it rang until it cracked. The event that took place afterwards is remembered as the 'First Libertarian Scream'.

    To commemorate the Independence of Chuquisaca and its 211 years.

  • Flickering lights

    Flickering lights is an ongoing activity initiated sometime in late-2018. The idea being to capture lights going through imperfections, short-circuits or any other sort of malfunction that makes them interact in a non-intentional way. Please do not watch if you are prompt to seizures, photosensitive or suffer from any conditions that may make you malfunction while watching this.

    Most of these videos are sporadically posted on my instagram @akiraishu
